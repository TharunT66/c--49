Interesting Facts About Camels

  • Camels are very well adapted to desert life and are one of teh most well known desert animal.
  • Camels don't store water in their humps. They only store fat to use when food is scarce.
  • Camels are clever animals with a good memory.

Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing colors is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons use their tail as a fifth limb to wrap around branches for balance and support.

Interesting Facts About Cicadas

  • There are more than 3000 species of cicadas.
  • Some cicada calls can be heard up to 1 mile.
  • Each species of cicada has a different song.
